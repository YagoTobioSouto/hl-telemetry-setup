You have £3,200 of ISA allowance left

The tax year ends at 23:59 on 5 April, and you've already used £16,800 of your £20,000 ISA allowance this year. That means you still have £3,200 available to invest tax-efficiently.

Any allowance you don't use will be lost – it doesn't carry forward to the next tax year. So if you'd like to make the most of your tax-free wrapper, now's the time to act.

Why top up your Stocks & Shares ISA?

When you invest in a Stocks & Shares ISA, any potential growth and income is shielded from UK Income Tax and Capital Gains Tax. The earlier you invest, the more time your money has to potentially grow.
